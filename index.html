I am a professional musician, playing primarily flute but also piano and voice. I have been playing flute since the age of 7 and have a Bachelor of Music in Flute Performance from McGill University.

I play for Contra Dances and English Country Dances all over the United States and Canada. Currently I play with Cojiro (Jess Newman and Mark Price), Les Quatre Patates (Jonathan Werk, Jacob deGroot-Maggetti, and Antonie Beaudet), and Modern Birds (Everest Witman and Jonathan Werk). I play for regular local dances as well as festivals like NEFFA and YDW.

I regularly host traditional music sessions around Montreal. You can often find me at the Monday night Québécois session at La Petite Marche or Sunday afternoon session at La Cale. I also sometimes host sessions at my home!

I play flute for the Royal Canadian Air Force as a reservist in the 438 Tactical Helicopter Squadron Band. We rehearse weekly and have several performances each month. I also occasionally deploy to locations around Canada to reinforce other bands or for special performances.
